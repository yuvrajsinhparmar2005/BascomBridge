Ethical Hacking Training FAQs

What is a Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification?
It is a professional certification for individuals who want to become skilled ethical hackers.
What does an ethical hacker do?
An ethical hacker identifies and helps fix security vulnerabilities in systems legally.
What are the benefits of enrolling in an ethical hacking certification course?
You gain industry-recognized skills, job opportunities, and hands-on experience in cybersecurity.
Is the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification worth it?
Yes, it boosts your career, salary potential, and job relevance in cybersecurity.
What are the career opportunities after earning a CEH certification?
Roles include penetration tester, security analyst, network security engineer, etc.
What certification will I receive after completing the CEH course?
You will receive the EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) certification.
How long does it take to complete the CEH course?
Typically, it takes about 40 hours of learning to complete the course.
Do I need prior experience for CEH certification?
Basic knowledge of networking and security is recommended, but not always required.
